Humidity


At first glance, it may look as though there is no clear relationship between latitude and humidity. It’s true that we observed 100% humidity at nearly every latitude, but focusing on the least humid cities we see a slight relationship…
If you are seeking somewhere very dry (between 0% to 20% humidity) then your best bet is actually to stay nearer the equator. Surely this doesn’t ensure a dry climate as there are many cities with very high humidity levels near the equator as well, but it seems that there is little to no chance you will find a completely dry city above or below +/-40° latitude. To prove this we would need to gather many more cities above and below said latitudes, and over many other days of the year surely, but it’s an interesting observation for this particular dataset.
